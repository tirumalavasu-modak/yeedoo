Yeedu acts as a federated metastore layer that reads from your existing systems directly. Instead of duplicating metadata, Yeedu connects to your Hive, AWS Glue, or Unity metastores instantly, preserving all governance, lineage, and compliance logs exactly where they are.

  • Personal Access Token authentication

  • Automatic connection URL extraction
  • Legacy Hadoop cluster support
